About Member Matcher

Member Matcher is a modern web application that intelligently connects community members based on complementary goals and industry alignment.

How it works:

  • Goal Complementarity (40%) - Matches people with complementary professional goals
  • Industry Alignment (30%) - Connects people in related industries
  • Role Complementarity (20%) - Pairs complementary roles
  • Location Proximity (10%) - Considers geographic proximity

Privacy First: All data processing happens in your browser - your data never leaves your device.
